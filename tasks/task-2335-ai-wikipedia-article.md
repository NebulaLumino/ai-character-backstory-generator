# Task 2335: AI Wikipedia Article & Encyclopedia Entry Generator
- **Status**: ✅ Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Writing, Publishing & Storytelling
- **App Dir**: ai-wikipedia-article
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-wikipedia-article
- **Description**: Generate Wikipedia-style encyclopedia articles with proper structure, neutral tone, and verifiable claims
- **Form Fields**: subject to write about, topic type (person/company/concept/event/city/technology), notable facts available, depth level (short-stub/medium/full-article), any controversial aspects to address neutrally, existing sources (or need for research)
- **Output**: Article with proper sections (lead/history/relevance/criticism/ reception/etc.), neutral tone throughout, inline citations (placeholders with guidance), infobox structure, "see also" links, external links section, image suggestion description, expansion priorities for longer article
