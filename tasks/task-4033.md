# Task 4033: Study Schedule Optimizer

**App Name:** ai-study-schedule-optimizer
**Priority:** HIGH
**Theme:** AI × Education, Learning, Research & Academic Tools

## App Description
An AI-powered study planner that creates personalized study schedules based on subjects, exam dates, and available hours. The AI weights priorities by difficulty, proximity to exam, and user performance to maximize study efficiency.

## Implementation Details
- Form inputs: list of subjects, exam dates, available hours per day/week, current proficiency levels
- Output: Daily/weekly study plan with time allocation, break reminders, and priority rankings
- UI: Dark gradient with indigo-500 accent
- API: DeepSeek via /api/generate route
- Build: `npx next build` must pass
