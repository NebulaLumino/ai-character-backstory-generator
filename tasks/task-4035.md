# Task 4035: Socratic Tutor

**App Name:** ai-socratic-tutor
**Priority:** HIGH
**Theme:** AI × Education, Learning, Research & Academic Tools

## App Description
An AI-powered Socratic tutor that guides students through questions rather than providing direct answers. Users input a topic or question, and the AI leads a guided dialogue using the Socratic method to deepen understanding and critical thinking.

## Implementation Details
- Form inputs: topic or question the student is struggling with, subject area, student's current level
- Output: Series of guided Socratic questions with hints and thought-provoking prompts
- UI: Dark gradient with indigo-500 accent
- API: DeepSeek via /api/generate route
- Build: `npx next build` must pass
