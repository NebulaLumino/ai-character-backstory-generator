# Task 4047: Essay Thesis Refiner

**App Name:** ai-essay-thesis-refiner
**Priority:** HIGH
**Theme:** AI × Education, Learning, Research & Academic Tools

## App Description
An AI tool that helps sharpen and focus essay thesis statements. Users input a draft thesis and paper direction, and the AI helps refine it into a clear, specific, and arguable claim with supporting angle.

## Implementation Details
- Form inputs: draft thesis statement, paper topic, target audience, essay type
- Output: Refined thesis with multiple stronger alternatives and explanations of improvements
- UI: Dark gradient with emerald-500 accent
- API: DeepSeek via /api/generate route
- Build: `npx next build` must pass
