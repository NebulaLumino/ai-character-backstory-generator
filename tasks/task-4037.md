# Task 4037: Research Paper Outline Generator

**App Name:** ai-research-paper-outline
**Priority:** HIGH
**Theme:** AI × Education, Learning, Research & Academic Tools

## App Description
An AI tool that generates comprehensive research paper outlines. Users provide a topic, thesis statement, and target journal, and the AI creates a structured outline including introduction, literature review, methodology, results, and discussion sections.

## Implementation Details
- Form inputs: research topic, thesis statement, target journal/field, paper length, methodology type
- Output: Full paper outline with section headers, subsections, and key points for each section
- UI: Dark gradient with cyan-500 accent
- API: DeepSeek via /api/generate route
- Build: `npx next build` must pass
