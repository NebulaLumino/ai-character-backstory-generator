# Task 2337: AI Writing Feedback & Developmental Edit Report Generator
- **Status**: ✅ Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Writing, Publishing & Storytelling
- **App Dir**: ai-writing-feedback
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-writing-feedback
- **Description**: Provide developmental editing feedback on writing samples — plot holes, pacing issues, character consistency, voice, and structural problems
- **Form Fields**: writing sample (excerpt or full description), genre, word count, intended audience, stage (draft 1/draft 2/final), specific concerns or areas to focus on
- **Output**: Overall assessment (strengths and weaknesses), plot/structure analysis, character consistency notes, pacing assessment, voice and tone evaluation, dialogue assessment, specific actionable feedback with page/chapter references, revision priority order (what to fix first), what is working well that should be preserved
