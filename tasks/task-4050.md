# Task 4050: Exam Countdown Planner

**App Name:** ai-exam-countdown-planner
**Priority:** HIGH
**Theme:** AI × Education, Learning, Research & Academic Tools

## App Description
An AI tool that creates countdown-based exam preparation schedules. Users input exam dates, syllabus coverage, and available hours, and the AI generates a day-by-day study plan with break reminders, priority weighting, and progress checkpoints.

## Implementation Details
- Form inputs: exam dates (multiple), syllabus topics with completion status, available hours per day
- Output: Countdown study schedule with daily tasks, break reminders, and milestone markers
- UI: Dark gradient with emerald-500 accent
- API: DeepSeek via /api/generate route
- Build: `npx next build` must pass
